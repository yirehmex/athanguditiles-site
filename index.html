YirehMex Athangudi tiles are traditional handmade decorative tiles originating from the Chettinad region of Tamil Nadu, India — renowned for their vibrant colors, intricate patterns and durability, crafted using a unique blend of local materials. Also known as Chettinad tiles, Heritage handmade tiles and Eco‑friendly tiles.

About YirehMex

Manufacturing, transportation & installation — end to end.

Elevate your space with authentic YirehMex Athangudi Tiles. Experience the timeless elegance of a craft that's been part of Chettinad homes for generations — add a touch of tradition to your interiors, or elevate your décor with handcrafted patterns made to order.
