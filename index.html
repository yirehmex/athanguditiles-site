    Manufacturing, Transportation & Installation  Services

    YirehMex Athangudi tiles are traditional handmade decorative tiles originating from the Chettinad region of Tamil Nadu, India. These tiles are renowned for their vibrant colors, intricate patterns, and durability. They are crafted using a unique blend of local materials. Athangudi tiles are also known as Chettinad tiles, Heritage handmade tiles and Eco-friendly tiles.
